Reading Reset

A six-week online training program for the parents of struggling readers aged 4-11.

  • How it works
  • How to book

Whether they’re in school or home-educated, if your child is aged 4-11 and they’re struggling with reading, this training is for you.

With support – and a little practise – you'll be able to help your child build reading skills and confidence.

We'll use an innovative approach called word mapping. It helps kids develop the things they don't get from established phonics: phonemic awareness, orthographic knowledge, motivation, and the ability to self-teach.

You’ll help them explore words of interest, discover the joy of reading, and rebuild the self belief that’s been squeezed out of them.

Working with Libby has opened my eyes to ways I can help my son.

Katherine O’Hanlon, Molesey

A six-week online support program

The approach

I'll teach you how to introduce your child to word mapping. This innovative, accessible approach to phonics, developed by Emma-Hartnell Baker, introduces kids to speech sounds using fun characters, which they love.

The format

The training is one-to-one, and all remote, using video calls and secure WhatsApp messages. I’ll help you to build skills and confidence while completing ten minutes of tasks with your child each day, Monday to Friday, for six weeks.

Outcomes

By the end of the training you will:

  • Better understand why reading and spelling have been challenging for your child.
  • Have developed skills in using a word-mapping approach with your child
  • Be empowered to support your child’s learning journey and advocate for their needs.
  • Feel optimistic because reading doesn’t have to be a battle – it can be something meaningful that you both enjoy.

Costs

The six-week reset is £1080. You’ll also need materials that cost £65.

Why now?

You don’t need to wait for a dyslexia diagnosis, or to do ‘more of the same’ at school if it isn’t working. For most kids, it doesn’t.
